About Quiz App


Learn Japanese N5 and N4 level words (Also kanji) with Quiz games and best stories. This app has separately each screen quiz for hiragana practice, kanji practice, grammar practice, and word meaning practice quiz. This app has challenging levels from 1 to 14 which has different rules in each level.
